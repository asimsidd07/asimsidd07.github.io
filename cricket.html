CRICKET

Professional

  • ID cards are mandatory for every

player who is playing.

  • For a player to play in the finals he

must play 1 or more matches in the

league stage.

• Players are requested to report

30mins before the scheduled time of

the match.

• Powerplay for a 15 over match is

4 overs and 20 overs match is 6 overs.


• Every player must wear coloured

jerseys.


• If the players (at least 9 members) are

not able to report in the given

scheduled time , an over will be

deducted for every 5 minutes and a

walkover will be given after 30min.


• Time limit to bowl 15 overs is 70min

and for 20 overs is 90 minutes.

If the time limit exceeds 70 mins

2 runs for given for every over.


NOTE : Time for drinks and injuries

will be excluded from the given slot.


• A bowler can bowl a maximum of

3 overs in 15 overs match and 4 overs

in a 20 overs match.

• Super subs are allowed and can only

be used once in the match

(Batting/Bowling)

• 15 members squad must.

• Cricheroes profile for every player is

must and that shall be done one

day prior to the start of tournament.

• If a player is found to be playing for

2 colleges, then both the team will be

disqualified.

• The playing 12 cannot be changed

after the toss.

• The matches will be played on a mat.

• Umpire’s decision final.
